Live Cross-Examination

Debate Partner

State a position. An opponent built to find its weakest point will attack — round by round, claim by claim.

What this is: most AI chat tools are agreeable by default. This one is built specifically to find holes in your thinking - attacking one claim at a time, tracking what you've defended or lost, and adapting its strategy round by round like a real debate opponent would.

How it works: each response from the AI carries both a natural-language rebuttal and structured data about the debate's state, generated in a single model call. That structured data drives the live scoreboard you see on the right.

Stack: Python, Flask, and the Groq API (Llama 3.3 70B) on the backend; vanilla JavaScript on the frontend - no frameworks.
